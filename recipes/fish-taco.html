Fish Tacos

Ingredients List

  • 1lb of tilapia
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 limes
  • 2 avocadoes
  • 1lb white flour
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 onion
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • Vegetable oil( to fry)
  • Tortillas
  • 2 tomatoes

Steps for Recipe

  1. Start with heating up a pan with vegetable oil at high heat.
  2. Prepare the batter by mixing flour, water, salt, pepper, and also mix in lime juice from one lime.
  3. Prepare the fish by using part of the flour to cover the fish and then cover it with the batter.
  4. Check to see if oil is hot enough by dropping some batter in it and if it floats and gets golden, the oil is ready.
  5. Take the fish and drop it away from you in the pan and making sure it stays afloat.
  6. Remove the fish from the pan once its crispy and golden, place on parchment paper to cool and remove excess oil.
  7. Prepare pico de gallo by cutting onion, serrano pepper,tomatoes, and avocadoes.Add a pich of salt.
  8. Heat tortillas and serve tacos.
