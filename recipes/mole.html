Pollo En Mole

Ingredients List

  • Jar of mole
  • Whole chicken(take apart) or quarter-legs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Poppy Seeds
  • Penut butter (optional)

Steps to Recipe

  1. Boil chicken in water till cooked
  2. Next add mole, salt, and pepper to chicken in pan.
  3. Cook for 30mins or till sauce becomes thick and darkened.
  4. Serves chicken with moles sauce on top add poppy seeds on top.
